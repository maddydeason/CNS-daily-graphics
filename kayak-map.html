Follow the Route: Circumnavigating the Delmarva Peninsula by Kayak

A decade after they first circumnavigated the Delmarva peninsula, Don Baugh, 61, and Tom Horton, 70, are kayaking 500 miles around the peninsula in 30 days to assess the health of local ecosystems.
